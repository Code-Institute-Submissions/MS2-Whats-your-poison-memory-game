Rules to follow while your here

  1. Pick your game difficulty setting

    Easy, 12 cards

    Medium, 20 cards

    Hard, 30 cards

  2. You may turn over 2 cards at a time, if they are a match they will remain face up. If they are not they will flip over again and you must remember where you left your drinks!

  3. The aim of the game is to match all the drinks in the quickest time possible to get your name on top of the scoreboard.
